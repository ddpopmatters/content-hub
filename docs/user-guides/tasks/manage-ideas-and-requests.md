## When To Use This

Use this guide when you need to capture future work before it becomes a scheduled content entry.

## Before You Start

- You need the relevant Ideas or Requests access.

## Steps

1. Open `Content`.
2. Switch to `Ideas` when you want to log or review content ideas.
3. Add a new idea when the work is not ready to become a planned entry.
4. Convert an idea into a content entry when it is ready for scheduling.
5. Switch to `Requests` when you need to review or manage content requests in the content workflow.

## What Happens Next

Ideas should move into scheduled work when they become actionable. Requests should move into the right delivery path rather than sitting indefinitely.

## Common Problems

### You cannot see Ideas or Requests

These sections are feature-dependent and may not be enabled for your account.

### The work is actually a landing page request

Use Pages Hub instead of keeping that workflow inside Content Hub.
