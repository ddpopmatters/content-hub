**Audience:** Content editors and planners  
**Owner:** TBD  
**Last reviewed:** 2026-03-30  
**Related guides:** `../quick-start.md`, `move-work-through-kanban.md`

## When To Use This

Use this workflow to review planned work, adjust dates, and manage upcoming content in the main calendar workspace.

## Before You Start

- You need calendar access.
- The content workspace must be available to your role.

## Steps

1. Open `Content`.
2. Select `Calendar` if another content tab is currently active.
3. Review the current month and planned items.
4. Open an entry when you need to update its details.
5. Create a new entry from `Add Content` or `Create content` if needed.
6. Adjust dates, status, or related details as part of planning.

## What Happens Next

Your entry remains in the shared planning flow and can then move through review and approval.

## Common Problems

### You cannot see the calendar

Your account may not have the required feature access.

### You are trying to plan a landing page request

Use Pages Hub for the landing page request lifecycle instead of forcing it into the content calendar.
