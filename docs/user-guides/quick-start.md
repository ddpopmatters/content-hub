## When To Use This

Use this guide when you are new to Content Hub or need the fastest route into the core workflow.

## Before You Start

- You need an active account and the relevant feature access.
- Your available tabs may differ depending on your permissions.

## First Three Things To Do

1. Open `Dashboard` and confirm you can see the main workspace.
2. Open `Content` and check which tabs are available to you.
3. Use `Add Content` to create a new item or open the `Calendar` tab to review scheduled work.

## Core Daily Flow

1. Review upcoming work in `Content`.
2. Create or update entries as needed.
3. Move items through the workflow from `Draft` toward `Published`.
4. If you are an approver, review assigned items in the approvals queue.
5. Use `Reporting` or `Insights` when you need performance or reporting views.

## Helpful Shortcuts

- `?` shows keyboard shortcuts.
- `n` starts a new content entry.
- `i` starts a new idea.
- `/` focuses search.
- `c` opens calendar view.
- `k` opens Kanban view.
- `a` opens approvals view.
- `d` opens ideas view.

## Common Early Problems

### You cannot see Calendar or Content creation

Your account may not have the calendar feature enabled.

### You expected Ideas or Requests but cannot see them

These areas are permission-based and may not be enabled for every user.
